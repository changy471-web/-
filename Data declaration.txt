Data Usage Statement

This dataset has been processed by acquisition equipment, with no additional pre-experimental processing performed. Users may independently conduct data interception, segmentation and secondary sorting according to the actual demands of experimental analysis, model construction and scientific research. In addition, plant electrical signals show prominent individual specificity and are easily affected by factors such as plant growth status, physiological differences and slight environmental fluctuations. Therefore, this batch of data only reflects the response characteristics of plant electrical signals under the established experimental environment and cultivation conditions of this study, and shall not be directly extrapolated or applied to other plant individuals or different experimental conditions.
